# Introduction
We research cognitive models and develop an eduation OS.

# Research Tracks

## Methodologies for flexible orchestration of multi-agents
By dynamically integrating the decisions of various sub-agents according to the specific situation, we aim to deliver AI outputs that capture the sophisticated human nuance, moving beyond rigid and uniform results.

## Automated Expertise Elicitation and Architecting
Transforming our manual process of deciphering and curating expert knowledge into an AI-driven distillation and pipelining process.

## Self-Improving HOTL Pipelines
Evolving our manual 'back-tracing' of expert feedback into an automated self-refining system.

# Vision
We envision a 'Cognition Decryption Engine'—a truly universal framework capable of deconstructing and architecting the intelligence for any specialized service with minimal human intervention.
